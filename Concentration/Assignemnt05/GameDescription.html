How to play Concentration

Game Rules:

Concentration is a card game that will test your memory and be bundles of fun!

Object of the Game

The goal of Concentration is to find the most matching pairs of cards.

Rules and Setup

  • Materials Needed for Play

    • Concentration can be played with a standard deck of cards, but can also be played with decks of cards that are designed especially for the game.

  • Number of Players

    • Concentration can be played solo, or it can be played with any number of people. It can be played as individuals or in teams.

  • Game Setup

    1. Shuffle the deck of cards
    2. Lay all of the cards on the table or playing surface face down.
    3. Decide who goes first if playing with others. (I recommend choosing whoever's birthday is soonest)
    4. The first player picks two cards and turns only those two cards over.
    5. If the cards match, the player removes them from the play area and sets the pair aside.
      If the cards do not match, they are turned back over and the next player chooses two cards.
    6. Play continues until all cards have been removed from play
